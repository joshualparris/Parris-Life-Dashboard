Boundary Road — Plan & Viewer Notes

This page documents the Boundary Road pano viewer and hosting plan.

Expected assets

  • /public/panos/boundary-road/*.jpg (360° equirectangular panos)
  • /public/panos/boundary-road/index.json (optional index with hotspots/meta)

How to publish

  1. Copy exported pano images into public/panos/boundary-road/.
  2. Add an index.json with hotspot positions if you want clickable points.
  3. Replace the placeholder at /games/boundary-road/index.html with a viewer (Pannellum/PhotoSphereViewer/etc.).

If you want, I can integrate a small Pannellum viewer and wire it to the panos folder — say the word and I'll add it.
